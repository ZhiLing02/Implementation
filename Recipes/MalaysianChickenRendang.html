Chicken Rendang Receipe & Tutorial



Ingredients

  • 100g Dry Chilli, Pre-Boiled
  • 500g Red Onion, Sliced
  • 80g Garlic, Sliced
  • 80g Ginger, Sliced
  • 130g Lemongrass, Sliced
  • 80g Galangal, Sliced
  • 40g Fresh Turmeric, Sliced

Other Ingredients

  • 1200g Pre-cut Whole Chicken, cut 12pcs
  • 250g Anchor UHT Extra Yield Cooking Cream
  • 300g Water
  • 30g Coconut Kerisik
  • 10g Turmeric Leaf, Sliced
  • 10g Fresh Lime Leaves
  • 15g Salt (to taste)
  • 8g Coarse Black Pepper (to taste)
  • 10g Fresh Turmeric Leaf, garnishing

How To Prepare :

  1. Blend all rendang paste ingredients until fine, set aside.
  2. Pour the blended rendang paste into large pot together with chicken, Anchor Extra Yield Cooking Cream, water, kerisik, turmeric leaves and lime leaves.
  3. Cook using medium heat for around 50 minutes until the chicken cooked and tender.
  4. Season with salt and course black pepper to taste.
  5. Just before turning off the heat, add in slices of fresh turmeric leaf to serve. Enjoy!

Servings :

12 pax


Total Time :

1 Hrs 20 Mins (Preparation time : 30 mins and Cook time : 50 mins)


Nutrition Facts :

Per Serving: 404 calories; protein 25g; carbohydrates 13g; fat 291g; cholesterol 85mg; sodium 81mg.
