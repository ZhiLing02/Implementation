    If a payment fails, you should receive an error notice on our checkout page that explains why. The following are some of the most prevalent reasons for payment failure:

    1. Insufficient funds
      2. Once your card has been topped up, you can attempt checking out again.
    2. Bank declined transaction
      3. This can only be resolve by contacting your card's issuing bank.
    3. 3D authentication failure
      4. Fail to enter the valid One Time Pin (OTP) to confirm payment. You may try checking out once more. If you are unable to get your OTP, please contact your respective bank.

    If you have any other payment concerns, please contact us at customer service with image of problem faced, and we will respond as soon as possible.
